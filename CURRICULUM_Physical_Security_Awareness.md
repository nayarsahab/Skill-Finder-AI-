# 📚 4-Week Learning Curriculum: PHYSICAL SECURITY AWARENESS

## 🔒 Safety Checklist & Guidelines
Before beginning, please read and follow these safety guidelines:

  1. Always adhere to local laws and property regulations.
  2. Do not engage in unauthorized testing of security systems.
  3. Maintain privacy of sensitive information.
  4. Prioritize personal safety in all assessment scenarios.
  5. Ensure all physical security practices comply with ethical guidelines.

## 📅 Week 1: Foundations: Understanding Physical Security Concepts and Terminology

### 📝 Learning Activities

  - Read introductory documentation on physical security definitions.
  - Watch introductory videos to understand the triad of deterrence, detection, and delay.
  - Research the role of physical security in overall organizational safety.

### 🔬 Practical Labs & Simulations

  - Conduct an audit of your own home to identify entry points, lock types, and visibility of assets.

### 🔗 Curated Resources

  - **Physical Security Awareness Reference and Guides (Wikipedia)** (Documentation): [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Physical%20Security%20Awareness](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Physical%20Security%20Awareness)

## 📅 Week 2: Guided Practice: Risk Assessment and Threat Identification

### 📝 Learning Activities

  - Complete online course modules focused on threat assessment.
  - Study the importance of lighting, signage, and surveillance cameras.
  - Learn how to create a basic physical security checklist.

### 🔬 Practical Labs & Simulations

  - Create a 'Security Walkthrough' document that evaluates the effectiveness of locks and window security in your workspace.

### 🔗 Curated Resources

  - **Learn Physical Security Awareness from Scratch (Coursera)** (Course): [https://www.coursera.org/search?query=Physical%20Security%20Awareness](https://www.coursera.org/search?query=Physical%20Security%20Awareness)

## 📅 Week 3: Applied Simulation: Developing Countermeasures and Workflow

### 📝 Learning Activities

  - Research common industry standards for visitor management and access control.
  - Observe how physical barriers such as fences and gates affect traffic flow.
  - Study video tutorials on proper incident response and reporting procedures.

### 🔬 Practical Labs & Simulations

  - Simulate an emergency evacuation drill for your home or office, timing how long it takes to secure items and exit.

### 🔗 Curated Resources

  - **Physical Security Awareness Beginner Video Course (YouTube)** (Video): [https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Physical%20Security%20Awareness+tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Physical%20Security%20Awareness+tutorial)

## 📅 Week 4: Capstone Project: Comprehensive Security Audit Report

### 📝 Learning Activities

  - Review all learned concepts regarding deterrence and monitoring.
  - Organize your audit findings into a structured report.
  - Draft recommendations for improving security at a selected location.

### 🔬 Practical Labs & Simulations

  - Develop and present a final 'Physical Security Audit Report' for your residence or office, detailing current vulnerabilities and proposing three actionable improvements.
