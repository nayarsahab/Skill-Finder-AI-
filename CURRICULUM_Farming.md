# 📚 4-Week Learning Curriculum: FARMING

## 🔒 Safety Checklist & Guidelines
Before beginning, please read and follow these safety guidelines:

  1. Always wear appropriate protective gear like gloves and sturdy footwear.
  2. Be aware of local weather conditions to prevent heat exhaustion.
  3. Use tools properly and keep them in good working condition.
  4. Wash hands thoroughly after handling soil or compost.
  5. Understand the safety guidelines for any fertilizers or tools you use.

## 📅 Week 1: Foundations: Understanding Soil and Plant Basics

### 📝 Learning Activities

  - Research the difference between soil types and nutrient needs.
  - Learn about hardiness zones and choosing appropriate crops for your region.
  - Explore basic safety guidelines for gardening tools.

### 🔬 Practical Labs & Simulations

  - Perform a simple soil texture test using a handful of soil and water.
  - Draw a layout plan for a small garden bed or container setup.

### 🔗 Curated Resources

  - **farming Reference and Guides (Wikipedia)** (Documentation): [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/farming](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/farming)

## 📅 Week 2: Guided Practice: Seeds, Planting, and Maintenance

### 📝 Learning Activities

  - Learn proper seed planting depths and spacing requirements.
  - Study the basics of irrigation and water management.
  - Identify common garden weeds and pests.

### 🔬 Practical Labs & Simulations

  - Start seeds indoors using biodegradable pots or small trays.
  - Create a watering schedule for your designated planting area.

### 🔗 Curated Resources

  - **farming Beginner Video Course (YouTube)** (Video): [https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=farming+tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=farming+tutorial)

## 📅 Week 3: Applied Simulation: Cultivation and Troubleshooting

### 📝 Learning Activities

  - Apply mulching techniques to regulate soil moisture.
  - Practice identifying healthy vs. stressed plant signs.
  - Learn natural pest control methods.

### 🔬 Practical Labs & Simulations

  - Transplant your seedlings into a larger container or small outdoor patch.
  - Execute a weekly garden inspection and logging task.

### 🔗 Curated Resources

  - **Learn farming from Scratch (Coursera)** (Course): [https://www.coursera.org/search?query=farming](https://www.coursera.org/search?query=farming)

## 📅 Week 4: Capstone Project: Growing and Harvesting

### 📝 Learning Activities

  - Develop a plan for long-term crop rotation.
  - Review harvesting best practices for your specific crops.

### 🔬 Practical Labs & Simulations

  - Manage your garden bed for one week, documenting progress and harvest.
  - Create a final report summarizing your findings, challenges, and successful growth metrics.
